About Us

Powering Campus Dining at Western Mindanao
State University

WM EAT SU is a digital food ordering platform built exclusively for the WMSU community. We connect hungry students with their favorite campus vendors — making it faster, easier, and more enjoyable to grab a meal between classes.

Our mission is to modernize campus dining by eliminating long queues, reducing food waste, and empowering student entrepreneurs and canteen vendors through technology.
